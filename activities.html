Evening Activities

Reflection & Symbolism

Evenings at Afterhaze are designed for reflection, calm observation and inner clarity.

Stone Meanings

  • Amethyst — clarity and calm thinking
  • Moonstone — intuition and reflection
  • Rose Quartz — emotional balance

Journaling Prompts

  • What did I notice today that I usually ignore?
  • What felt heavy, and what felt light?
  • What am I ready to let go of?

Fireside Mocktails

Rose Cream Mist

Rose syrup, cream (or coconut milk), vanilla foam, and a soft lemongrass note. Smooth and floral.

Ember Raspberry Chili

Raspberry puree, soda, lemon juice and a light chili infusion. Sweet heat with a mountain fire vibe.

Lemongrass Aloe Spritz

Lemongrass syrup, aloe soda, lime and ice. A refreshing forest-air inspired drink.
